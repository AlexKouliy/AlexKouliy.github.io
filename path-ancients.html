Deep Lore

Enter a world shaped by the eternal struggle between Storm and Void, primordial forces from long ago. The Arena of the Forgotten stands as a testament to the ancient civilization's hubris, where they sought to control the very forces of creation. Now, in this shattered realm, champions harness these primal energies, each card and ability echoing the wisdom and warnings of a fallen age.

The Storm, born of unbearable emotion, brings both creative chaos and destructive power. The Void, emerging from the desperate need for control, offers order but demands sacrifice. Between these forces walk the Edgewalkers, ancient guides who understand that true power comes not from dominance, but from balance.

The Arena

The Arena of the Forgotten is more than a battlefield, it is a living echo of the ancient world's fall. Here, where the boundaries between Storm and Void grow thin, champions face not only each other but the very forces that shaped reality. Will you embrace the Storm's chaos, submit to the Void's control, or find your own balance between these timeless forces?
